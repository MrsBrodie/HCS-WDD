At Springfield Zoo, our most famous animal is Jacob the Sumatran Tiger!

Tigers are globally listed as “endangered”; the Sumatran sub-species is listed as “critically endangered.” Wild tigers are hunted to meet the demands of the $20 billion a year illegal wildlife market.

When you adopt one of our Sumatran tigers you will be helping to pay for the care, feeding and general well-being of that animal for one year. The costs of running a zoo are enormous and every penny contributed makes a huge difference.

Adoption costs £49.99 and if you sign-up you will receive:

  • A certificate of adoption
  • A picture of your chosen animal
  • Five complimentary adult tickets to Springfield Zoo
  • A fact sheet about your animal

Celebrate the big day with friends and family in one of our private party locations throughout the zoo and we’ll help you create your unique two-hour birthday bash. Valid zoo membership or zoo admission tickets are required for all party guests, which allows them the opportunity to visit all the amazing animals before and after your party.

Numbers and cost

The minimum number of people required to book a party is 10 and the cost is £22 per person.

When are parties offered?

You can book one of our party rooms for a 2-hour slot on weekends (10am-12pm, 12pm-2pm, 2pm-4pm).

When should I book my party?

Due to high demand, we recommend booking as early as 2-3 months in advance. Parties cannot be booked any less than 4 weeks in advance. Call 0192 611 5391 or e-mail us at info@springfieldzoo.co.uk to submit your request.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

Yes, a non-refundable deposit of £11 per person is required when you book your party. The total balance must be paid 2 weeks prior to the big day. If you book the party within a month of the date, the full amount will be due to reserve the spot.

What is provided?

Safari themed-decorations, tablecloths, paper goods, serving knife, and matches are all provided in the party space.

Can I choose which animals come to the party space?

Our animal collection is subject to availability and there is no guarantee a specific animal can come into the space. However, you will be able to choose from themed animal categories and 2-3 animals will be chosen from the category you pick.
